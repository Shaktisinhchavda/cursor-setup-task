## Format Pattern

Wistia mixes product updates, integration-focused education, and video repurposing guidance. This shows how a B2B video company can use YouTube both to educate the market and to explain product workflows.

## Audience

- B2B marketers
- content teams
- video marketers
- lifecycle and demand generation teams

## Why This Is Useful For B2B SaaS

The channel connects video production to business workflows: integrations, repurposing, automation, and marketing execution. That makes the content more practical than generic brand awareness video.

## Playbook Lesson

Product-led videos work better when they are tied to an operational use case, such as turning viewers into customers or repurposing long-form video into short clips.

## Pillar Match

- Product-led education
- Repurposable interviews / source assets
- Search-led tutorials

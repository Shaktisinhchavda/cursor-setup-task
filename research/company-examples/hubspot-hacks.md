## Format Pattern

HubSpot Hacks uses specific, workflow-led tutorials. The videos are framed around jobs inside HubSpot rather than broad marketing advice.

## Audience

- HubSpot admins
- marketers using HubSpot
- RevOps and marketing ops teams
- teams trying to implement CRM or marketing automation workflows

## Why This Is Useful For B2B SaaS

This is a strong example of search-led product education. The titles are specific enough that buyers or users can understand exactly what problem the video solves before clicking.

## Playbook Lesson

A SaaS channel should not only publish thought leadership. It should also answer high-intent workflow questions that buyers, users, and evaluators search for.

## Pillar Match

- Search-led tutorials
- Product-led education

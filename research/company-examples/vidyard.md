## Format Pattern

Vidyard combines product education, sales enablement, and expert-led content. Its metadata points to a channel built around video as a revenue workflow, not just content marketing.

## Audience

- sales teams
- demand generation teams
- revenue leaders
- marketers using video across the funnel

## Why This Is Useful For B2B SaaS

Vidyard is useful because it frames video as part of pipeline creation and buyer communication. That helps avoid the common mistake of treating YouTube as only a brand awareness channel.

## Playbook Lesson

B2B SaaS YouTube content should include sales-supporting assets, not just top-of-funnel educational videos.

## Pillar Match

- Product-led education
- Expert point-of-view videos
- Sales enablement content

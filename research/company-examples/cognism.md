## Format Pattern

Cognism publishes operator-led and conversation-led content around sales, RevOps, and go-to-market problems. The topics are framed around real GTM pain points rather than product features alone.

## Audience

- BDR and SDR leaders
- RevOps teams
- demand generation teams
- sales and revenue leaders

## Why This Is Useful For B2B SaaS

Cognism shows how a SaaS company can use YouTube to own conversations around the problems its buyers already care about: signal quality, reply rates, outbound effectiveness, and RevOps.

## Playbook Lesson

Topic selection should come from buyer pain and sales conversations. A product does not need to be the explicit subject of every video for the content to support demand generation.

## Pillar Match

- Expert point-of-view videos
- Repurposable interviews
- Category education

# First 90 Days Calendar

Date: 2026-06-16

This is a practical publishing test plan for a B2B SaaS YouTube channel. It assumes a small team and one subject-matter expert.

## Month 1: Foundation And Learning

Goal:

- Validate topics, format, and workflow without overproducing.

Videos:

1. Search-led tutorial: one urgent buyer problem
2. Category POV: why the problem matters now
3. Product-led education: one workflow walkthrough
4. Expert interview: internal SME or friendly customer/operator

Distribution checklist:

- Publish each video on YouTube
- Create 2 LinkedIn posts from each video
- Create 2 short clips from each video
- Add relevant videos to sales follow-up snippets
- Add tutorial videos to relevant website/help pages

Review:

- Which topics created qualified comments or sales conversations?
- Which videos had the best retention?
- Which clips performed best on LinkedIn?
- Which assets did sales actually use?

## Month 2: Repeat What Shows Signal

Goal:

- Double down on topics and formats that produced useful audience signals.

Videos:

1. Search-led tutorial based on Month 1 comments/questions
2. Search-led tutorial based on sales/customer success questions
3. Category POV connected to market shift or industry debate
4. Expert interview with customer, partner, or operator

Distribution checklist:

- Turn best Month 1 video into a blog/resource page
- Turn one expert interview into 5 clips
- Ask sales for 5 questions prospects keep asking
- Add one video to onboarding or nurture

Review:

- Are viewers from the right audience?
- Are comments/questions becoming more specific?
- Are sales/customer success teams reusing the content?
- Are search-led videos producing durable views?

## Month 3: Build A Repeatable System

Goal:

- Convert early learning into a predictable operating cadence.

Videos:

1. Search-led tutorial for highest-intent query
2. Product-led education for a key evaluation workflow
3. Category POV from founder or senior SME
4. Expert interview or teardown

Distribution checklist:

- Build a recurring repurposing workflow
- Document standard video briefs
- Build a dashboard by content pillar
- Create a backlog of 20 validated topics

Review:

- Which pillar should become the core channel identity?
- Which format is easiest to produce consistently?
- Which videos influence sales/customer conversations?
- Which topics should become a series?

## 90-Day Success Criteria

This program is working if:

- The team has published 10 to 12 useful videos
- Sales or customer success has reused at least 3 videos
- At least one topic format has clear retention or engagement signal
- The team has a documented topic backlog
- YouTube content is being repurposed into LinkedIn, email, web, and sales assets

# Final README Notes

Use these notes when polishing the README before final submission.

## Suggested Final Summary

This repository researches YouTube content strategy for B2B SaaS. It studies practitioners and companies that connect video with demand generation, content distribution, sales enablement, product education, and founder-led trust building.

## What Was Collected

- 10 expert/source shortlist
- public YouTube channel metadata
- recent YouTube RSS upload metadata
- transcript target list
- transcript collection attempt logs
- public source notes from Foundation and Vidyard
- SaaS YouTube channel examples
- synthesis artifacts for pillars, measurement, distribution, and first 90 days

## Why These Experts Were Chosen

The experts were selected because they represent different parts of a complete B2B SaaS YouTube strategy:

- content distribution
- demand generation
- SaaS product education
- video marketing
- content repurposing
- founder-led go-to-market education
- company-led YouTube programming

## Known Limitations

- LinkedIn post collection still requires manual or authenticated access.

- The draft playbook should be treated as a working synthesis until more primary source text is collected.

# Content Pillar Matrix

Date: 2026-06-16

This matrix translates the research so far into repeatable YouTube programming options for a B2B SaaS company.

## Pillar 1: Search-Led Tutorials

Purpose:

- Capture buyers who are already searching for a workflow, problem, or tool-specific answer.

Best topics:

- How to solve a specific workflow problem
- How to configure an integration
- How to compare two approaches
- How to use a feature to get a business outcome
- Common mistakes in a workflow

Example titles:

- How to Build a Lead Scoring Model in [Tool]
- How to Fix Low Demo Conversion From Paid Search
- [Tool A] vs [Tool B]: Which Is Better for RevOps Teams?
- How to Create a Weekly Pipeline Report Your VP Sales Will Actually Use

Distribution:

- Embed in help docs and product pages
- Send from sales/customer success
- Repurpose into LinkedIn carousel or short clips
- Turn transcript into an SEO article

Primary metrics:

- YouTube search impressions
- watch time
- product page clicks
- support-ticket reduction
- sales/customer success usage

## Pillar 2: Category POV

Purpose:

- Shape how buyers understand the market, the problem, and the need for change.

Best topics:

- Why the old way no longer works
- What buyers misunderstand about the category
- Market shifts affecting the ICP
- Opinionated breakdowns of common advice
- Strategic lessons from operators

Example titles:

- Why MQLs Are Not Enough for Modern SaaS Demand Generation
- The Real Reason Your SaaS Content Is Not Creating Pipeline
- What AI Search Changes About B2B Content Strategy
- Why Product Demos Should Start Before the Demo Call

Distribution:

- Founder or SME LinkedIn post
- Newsletter section
- Sales enablement note
- Podcast discussion topic
- Community discussion prompt

Primary metrics:

- comments from target buyers
- saves and shares
- newsletter signups
- sales mentions
- qualitative feedback

## Pillar 3: Product-Led Education

Purpose:

- Show how the product helps solve a real business problem without making the video feel like a generic demo.

Best topics:

- Use-case walkthroughs
- Before/after workflow breakdowns
- Feature combinations
- Role-specific product workflows
- Customer implementation examples

Example titles:

- How a RevOps Manager Can Build a Clean Handoff Workflow in 20 Minutes
- How to Use [Product] to Prioritize Accounts Showing Buying Intent
- From Spreadsheet to Dashboard: Fixing Forecast Reporting With [Product]

Distribution:

- Product pages
- onboarding emails
- lifecycle campaigns
- sales follow-up
- help center articles

Primary metrics:

- demo-page engagement
- activation support
- trial-to-paid movement
- sales usage
- customer education usage

## Pillar 4: Repurposable Expert Interviews

Purpose:

- Create a long-form source asset that can feed YouTube, LinkedIn, email, clips, and sales enablement.

Best topics:

- Operator interviews
- Customer problem walkthroughs
- Partner expert sessions
- Internal SME breakdowns
- Teardowns of real examples

Example titles:

- How a VP Marketing Rebuilt Their Content Engine After Organic Traffic Dropped
- A RevOps Leader Breaks Down Their Pipeline Review Process
- How This SaaS Team Turns Webinars Into 30 Days of Content

Distribution:

- Full YouTube episode
- short clips
- quote graphics
- newsletter
- blog recap
- sales snippets

Primary metrics:

- clip output
- partner shares
- email clicks
- influenced conversations
- account-level engagement

## Recommended Starting Mix

For the first 90 days:

| Pillar | Frequency | Why |
|---|---:|---|
| Search-led tutorials | 2 per month | Builds durable discovery and useful assets |
| Category POV | 2 per month | Builds trust and point of view |
| Product-led education | 1 per month | Helps sales, onboarding, and evaluation |
| Expert interviews | 1 per month | Creates source material for repurposing |
